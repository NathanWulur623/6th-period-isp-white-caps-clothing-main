What's In Our Clothes

100% Organic Cotton


Our garments are made going to made from 100% organic cotton, which means the cotton is not genetically modified. Organic cotton is grown only with natural rainwater avoiding any harmful chemicals that contribute toward pollution


Mission: Today, the fashion industry procudes 20% of global water pollution. Our goal as White Caps is to be a brand that doesn't contribute to this waste and provide quality garments made from sustainable materials. We will be giving back to our environment by donating 30% of our sales towards charities such as Greenpeace, Oceana, and the Surfrider Foundation.
